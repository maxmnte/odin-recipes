Marinated Grapes

Already a hit last summer, these grapes will provide plenty of refreshment again this year.

Ingredients

For 4 persons / 15 Minutes

  • 300 g Seedless grapes
  • 40 g Sugar
  • 50 ml Water (or sparkling wine)
  • some lime juice
  • some Tajín spice (depending on preference)

Steps

  1. Before "marinating" the grapes, wash them thoroughly and pat them dry.
  2. Then mix the water, sugar – alternatively, sparkling wine is also suitable as a fancy substitute for water – and a dash of lime juice in a separate bowl and toss the grapes in it.
  3. Depending on taste, the still slightly moist grapes can be rolled in a mixture of sugar and Tajín spice before freezing until they are evenly coated.
  4. Then spread the marinated grapes on a plate or baking tray lined with baking paper – preferably so that they do not touch. Then place in the freezer and leave to chill for at least four hours, ideally overnight.
  5. When the fruits are frozen, they can be enjoyed as a light summer or party snack.
